MSc Spatial Engineering · ITC Faculty · University of Twente
Digital Twins for Smart Buildings

This thesis investigates how digital twin technologies can be applied to smart building management systems to enable continuous, data-driven monitoring of energy performance and indoor environmental conditions. The research addresses the full pipeline — from heterogeneous IoT sensor data acquisition and harmonisation, through machine learning-based energy forecasting, to integration within an interactive 3D geospatial digital twin platform.

A key contribution is a reproducible sensor-to-API data infrastructure developed at the GATE Institute in Sofia — replicating institutional circuit-level electricity and environmental sensor data, harmonising multi-rate time-series onto a consistent 5-minute grid, and exposing processed datasets via a PostgREST API layer connected to a CesiumJS 3D building frontend.
